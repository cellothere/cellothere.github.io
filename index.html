Privacy Policy for Musical Passport

Effective date: April 9, 2026

Musical Passport ("Musical Passport," "we," "us," or "our") provides a music discovery app that helps users explore artists, genres, and songs from around the world.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, when we share it, and the choices available to you when you use the Musical Passport mobile app and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information depending on how you use the app:

  • Spotify account information if you connect Spotify, including your Spotify user ID, display name, email address, top artists, top tracks, and related authorized data.
  • Apple Music information if you connect Apple Music, including your Apple Music user token and library artist data used to generate recommendations and insights.
  • Favorites, saved tracks, stamps, visited countries, genres explored, and similar in- app activity.
  • Basic operational data such as app requests, errors, logs, and device-local storage needed to run the app.
  • Third-party music metadata and media-related information from services such as Spotify, Apple Music, Deezer, YouTube, Last.fm, ListenBrainz, MusicBrainz, and Discogs.

2. How We Use Information

  • To authenticate your Spotify or Apple Music connection.
  • To provide recommendations, artist lookups, previews, and personalized insights.
  • To save favorites, stamps, and account-linked activity.
  • To operate, secure, maintain, and improve the app.

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information with music/content providers you connect to, infrastructure and storage providers, service providers that support app functionality, and legal authorities when required.

4. Data Storage and Retention

We store some information on our servers and some locally on your device. Server-side data may include your Spotify user ID, display name, top artists, favorites, stamps, and cached insight results. We retain data as reasonably necessary to provide and protect the service.

5. Notifications

If you allow notifications, Musical Passport may schedule local notifications on your device, including reminders related to the country-of-the-day feature. You can disable notifications in device settings at any time.

6. Third-Party Services

Third-party services such as Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube, Deezer, and Supabase may have their own privacy policies and terms.

7. Your Choices

  • You can choose whether to connect Spotify or Apple Music.
  • You can revoke access through your Spotify or Apple account settings.
  • You can request deletion of account-linked stored data by contacting us.

8. Children's Privacy

Musical Passport is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards, but no system is completely secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above.
