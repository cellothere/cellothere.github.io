Privacy Policy for Musical Passport

Effective date: April 9, 2026

Musical Passport ("Musical Passport," "we," "us," or "our") provides a music discovery app that helps users explore artists, genres, and songs from around the world.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, when we share it, and the choices available to you when you use the Musical Passport mobile app and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information depending on how you use the app:

Information you provide or authorize

  • Favorites and saved content, such as tracks, countries, and other items you save in the app.
  • Passport and discovery activity, such as stamps, visited countries, genres explored, and related in-app interactions.
  • Optional feedback or review data if you report a problem with a track or similar content.

Information collected automatically

  • App usage and request data, such as requests made to our backend, basic error information, and service logs needed to operate, secure, and improve the app.
  • Device-local app data stored on your device, such as tester status, search history, saved app state, and scheduled local notifications.

Third-party content data

  • Music metadata and media-related information from third-party services such as Spotify, Apple Music, Deezer, YouTube, Last.fm, ListenBrainz, MusicBrainz, and Discogs, including artist names, track names, artwork, preview URLs, and related catalog metadata.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • save your favorites, stamps, and other account-linked activity
  • play available previews and open supported third-party music services
  • operate, secure, debug, maintain, and improve the app and backend
  • prevent abuse, fraud, or misuse of the service

3. Legal Bases and Permissions

Where applicable, we process information based on:

  • your consent, including when you enable notifications
  • performance of the service you request when using the app
  • our legitimate interests in operating, securing, and improving Musical Passport

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information with:

  • music and content providers you choose to access through the app, including Spotify, Apple Music, Deezer, and YouTube
  • infrastructure and data storage providers that help us operate the service, including Supabase and hosting providers
  • service providers and APIs used to generate recommendations, enrich metadata, or support app functionality, such as Anthropic, Last.fm, ListenBrainz, MusicBrainz, and Discogs
  • legal authorities or other parties when required by law, to protect users, or to enforce our rights

We may also share aggregated or de-identified information that does not reasonably identify you.

5. Data Storage and Retention

We store some information on our servers and some information locally on your device.

Server-side stored data may include:

  • your saved favorites
  • your stamps and visit counts
  • limited operational logs and cached recommendation data

We retain data for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the app, maintain account- linked features, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Locally stored data may remain on your device until you delete the app or clear app storage.

6. Notifications

If you allow notifications, Musical Passport may schedule local notifications on your device, such as reminders related to the "country of the day" feature.

We do not currently state here that we collect or store push notification tokens unless and until that behavior is added to the app.

You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

7. Third-Party Services

Musical Passport relies on third-party services and content sources. Their own privacy policies and terms may apply to your use of their services. We encourage you to review them directly, including:

  • Spotify
  • Apple Music
  • YouTube
  • Deezer
  • Supabase

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services we do not control.

8. Your Choices

You can:

  • disable notifications in your device settings
  • stop using the app at any time
  • request deletion of your account-linked stored data by contacting us at the address below

If you request deletion, we will take reasonable steps to delete or de-identify data associated with your account, subject to legal, security, fraud-prevention, backup, and recordkeeping needs.

9. Children's Privacy

Musical Passport is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to review and delete it where applicable.

10. International Use

If you use Musical Passport outside the country where our services are operated, your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your own, which may have different data protection laws.

11. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we may update the effective date above and take other appropriate steps consistent with applicable law.
